Recipes

See below for some of my favorite receipes and resources

  • Hershey's Cookbook - my family had this cookbook when I was growing up and this is how I got my start. I still get requests from my family for the 'Deep Dark Chocolate Cake' from this book!
  • Cake Magic Cookbook - my friend bought me this book recently. It has tons of cake, syrup, and frosting recipes that you can mix and match for interesting flavor combinations.
  • Pinterest - great for inspiration as well as recipes and decorating guides.
  • Orange Cranberry and White Chocolate Scones - scones are one of my favorite things to throw together quickly and this receipe is always a hit. It also features a method that doesn't require specialized tools, just a normal grater.
  • Raspberry Almond Shortbread Cookies - I made these for the holidays and they were a huge hit!
